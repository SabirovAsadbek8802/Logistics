How long does it take to ship a car ?

It will generally take between a few days and two weeks for your car to arrive at its pickup point after it heads out for transport. This window is extremely wide because auto transport times vary considerably based on the individual shipment.If your car is heading just a few hundred miles away, it could take as little as a day to receive it. For long-distance car transports like from California to an East Coast location like New York or Florida, expect the shipment to take about a week. Beyond that, auto carriers could take a week or more to get your vehicle to spots like Hawaii, Alaska or international locations.

Can I ship my car with my personal belongings ?

You’ll need to secure your personal belongings so they aren’t moving around the vehicle during the transport, potentially damaging the vehicle. Remember, as I mentioned earlier, damage done to the vehicle as a result of your personal belongings is not covered by the carrier’s cargo insurance. We recommend you put your stuff in suitcases, duffle bags, or boxes and then make sure they are secured and aren’t going to move around easily.
